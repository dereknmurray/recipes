# Vinaigrettes

## Classic Dijon Vinaigrette

### Ingredients
* 1/2 C White White Vinegar
* 2 T Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard
* 4 Clv Garic, minced
* 1 1/4 C Olive Oil
* 1/4 t White Pepper

### Preparation
Blend first 4 ingredients in blender until well blended. Add oil and pepper and blend for 15 seconds.
